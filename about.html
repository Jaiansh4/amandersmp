About Us

Amader SMP is a Bangladeshi 24/7 Minecraft survival server focused on community, performance, and fun. We support both Java and Bedrock players and run regular events, seasonal updates, and community projects.

What we offer

  • High-Spec Performance — Dedicated hosting and optimized plugins for a smooth experience.
  • Custom Features — Homes, backpacks, kits, and quality-of-life commands like /craft and /recipe.
  • Active Community — Friendly Bangladeshi players, staff-run events, and community projects.
  • Support — Open tickets on Discord for purchases, help, and appeals.
